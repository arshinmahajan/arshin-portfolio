THE PROBLEM

Operational Reporting Is Slow

Operations teams spend significant time extracting reports, creating spreadsheets, building pivot tables and investigating unexpected cost increases. By the time insights are identified, the business impact has often already occurred. Leaders need answers immediately.

PRODUCT VISION

Conversational Operations Analytics

Instead of navigating dashboards, users simply ask questions. The AI assistant analyzes operational data, identifies patterns, explains anomalies and provides actionable insights. The goal is to reduce time-to-insight from hours to seconds.

Why Root Cause Analysis?

Why Executive Summaries?
