PRODUCT CASE STUDY

AI Operations Intelligence

An AI-powered Operations Analytics Copilot that helps leaders understand operational cost, identify anomalies and uncover root causes through natural language conversations.

The Problem

Enterprise operations teams spend hours every week manually exporting reports, building spreadsheets, creating pivot tables and investigating cost spikes. By the time insights are discovered, the business impact has already occurred.

Operations leaders need answers immediately:

  • Why did costs increase?
  • Which region is underperforming?
  • What caused the anomaly?
  • What is the business impact?

Product Vision

Transform operational reporting from a manual process into a conversational experience. Instead of building reports, leaders simply ask questions. The AI agent performs the analysis, identifies root causes and presents actionable insights.

Core User Journeys

KPI Summarisation

"What are total costs broken down by region?" The AI instantly aggregates and compares performance across business units.

Anomaly Investigation

"What happened to EMEA costs in November?" The assistant identifies anomalies, root causes and business impact.

Executive Reporting

Generate executive-ready summaries without analyst intervention.

Operational Visibility

Track performance, SLA breaches and operational cost drivers.

The assistant converts natural language into operational analysis and presents business-ready insights in seconds.

Product Decisions

Why conversational analytics? Most operational leaders do not think in SQL, filters or dashboard configuration. They think in business questions. The product was designed around natural language to reduce friction and increase adoption.

Why focus on root-cause analysis? Most dashboards describe what happened. Few explain why it happened. The product prioritizes causal reasoning to support faster decision-making.
