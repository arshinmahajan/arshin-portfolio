THE PROBLEM

Businesses Have Data, Not Direction

Most organizations collect hundreds of metrics, yet struggle to identify which KPI actually drives success. Teams spend days creating dashboards, building reports and debating priorities. Decision makers need a faster way to move from: Raw Data → Insight → Action.

PRODUCT VISION

An AI Analyst For Every Team

NorthStar AI Agent acts as an analytics strategist. Users upload a dataset and receive: • Executive dashboards • KPI recommendations • North Star Metric discovery • AI-generated insights • Conversational analytics within seconds.
